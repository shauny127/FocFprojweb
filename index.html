This course offers an introduction to the core principles of Computer Science, providing students with a solid foundation for more advanced studies in the field. It presents a broad survey of key topics, enabling students to grasp the full scope of computer systems and their capabilities. Areas of focus include computer architecture, operating systems, computer networks, software development, data organization and visualization, and the vast potential of computing technologies.
